Welcome to La Woods, Tiruvanamalai - Your Gateway to Tiruvanamalai's Cultural Delights!
Administrator
22.09.2017
4 comments

Explore the rich heritage and vibrant culture of Tiruvanamalai while enjoying your stay at La Woods, Tiruvanamalai. Located in the heart of the city, our hotel offers easy access to some of the most fascinating museums, iconic landmarks, and serene attractions that Tiruvanamalai has to offer. Immerse yourself in the history, art, and natural beauty of this bustling metropolis. Here are some of the must-visit attractions near our hotel:

Sri Ramanashramam: A Spiritual Sanctuary of Inner Peace

Sri Ramanashramam is a serene and revered spiritual ashram located at the foothills of the sacred Arunachala mountain in Tiruvannamalai, Tamil Nadu, India. Founded in the early 20th century by the renowned sage Bhagavan Sri Ramana Maharshi, the ashram has become a beacon of spiritual wisdom, attracting seekers from all corners of the globe.

The ashram is dedicated to the teachings and legacy of Sri Ramana Maharshi, who emphasized self-inquiry as a means to realize the true nature of the Self. Visitors come to Sri Ramanashramam seeking solace, guidance, and profound introspection on the path to self-discovery and enlightenment.

Javadu Hills: Exploring Nature's Bounty

Scenic Beauty: Javadu Hills is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, dense forests, and rolling hills. The lush greenery and serene ambiance make it a perfect destination for nature lovers and photographers.

Trekking and Hiking: Adventure enthusiasts can embark on thrilling trekking and hiking trails that lead to vantage points offering mesmerizing views of the surrounding hills and valleys.

Parvathamalai Mountain: A Sacred Trek to Spiritual Heights

Parvathamalai Mountain stands majestically in the scenic landscape of Tamil Nadu, India, exuding an aura of profound spirituality. This ancient mountain has been a revered pilgrimage site for seekers and devotees alike, offering a transformative journey of self-discovery and communion with the divine.

Located near the town of Polur, Parvathamalai is steeped in mythological and historical significance. It is believed to be the abode of the divine mother, Goddess Parvathi, hence its name, which translates to "Mountain of Parvathi." According to local legends, several sages and saints have sought solace and enlightenment on its slopes, infusing the place with spiritual energy.

Sathanur Dam Park: Where Nature and Leisure Embrace

Sathanur Dam Park, located near the iconic Sathanur Dam in Tamil Nadu, India, is a delightful retreat that harmoniously blends nature's charm with leisurely pursuits. Situated within the lush landscapes of the dam's surroundings, the park offers visitors a serene escape to unwind, reconnect with nature, and enjoy an array of recreational activities.

Nestled on the banks of the tranquil Pennaiyar River, Sathanur Dam Park welcomes visitors with its picturesque vistas and refreshing ambiance. The park's carefully curated gardens, adorned with colorful blooms and well-manicured lawns, provide the perfect setting for families, friends, and solo travelers to immerse themselves in the beauty of nature.

Virupaksha Cave: A Sacred Sanctuary of Spiritual Contemplation

Nestled within the rocky outcrops of the sacred Arunachala mountain in Tiruvannamalai, Tamil Nadu, India, the Virupaksha Cave holds a hallowed place in the hearts of spiritual seekers. This ancient cave, steeped in mystical significance, is renowned for being the abode of the revered sage, Sri Ramana Maharshi, during his early years of spiritual awakening.

Virupaksha Cave, also known as Virupaksha Gufa or Virupaksha Hill Cave, is a place of profound tranquility and contemplation. As visitors ascend the rocky path to reach the cave's entrance, they are greeted by the serene surroundings, emanating a sense of timeless spirituality.
