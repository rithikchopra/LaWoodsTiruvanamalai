Activites

Girivalam:

Embark on the Girivalam, a sacred ritual of circumambulating the Arunachala Hill. This 14-kilometer walk around the hill is believed to bestow spiritual blessings and cleanse one's soul.

Karthigai Deepam Festival:

If you visit during the Karthigai Deepam festival, you can witness the grand lighting of a huge lamp atop the Arunachala Hill, creating a mesmerizing spectacle.

Meditation and Yoga:

Tiruvannamalai is known as a spiritual hub, attracting seekers from around the world. Engage in meditation and yoga practices at various ashrams and meditation centers.

Ramana Ashram:

Visit the famous Sri Ramana Maharshi Ashram, dedicated to the renowned sage Ramana Maharshi. It is a serene place for meditation and self-reflection.

Seshadri Swamigal Ashram:

Explore the ashram of Seshadri Swamigal, a revered saint who lived in Tiruvannamalai during the 19th century.

Annamalaiyar Temple Walk:

Stroll through the temple's corridors and witness the vibrant rituals performed by devotees.

Cultural Events:

Attend cultural programs and religious discourses held in and around the temple premises.
